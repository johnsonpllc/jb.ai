This Week's Tip

Every missed call could be costing your small business more than you think.

  • **Calculate your average customer value** - Multiply your typical sale amount by how many times customers return, then factor in referrals they bring.

  • **Track your missed call rate** - Most small businesses miss 30-50% of incoming calls during busy periods or after hours, meaning potential revenue walking away daily.

  • **Multiply the pain** - If you miss 10 calls weekly and just 2 would convert to customers worth $500 each, that's $52,000 in lost annual revenue.

  • **AI voice bots answer every call** - They capture lead information 24/7, qualify prospects, and book appointments even when you're unavailable.

  • **ROI adds up fast** - A voice bot costing $200/month pays for itself by converting just one additional lead monthly.

  • **Start with your busiest hours** - Deploy AI during peak times when you're most likely to miss calls.
