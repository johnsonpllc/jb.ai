This Week's Tip

Calculating your true lead cost is crucial for measuring AI voice bot ROI and optimizing your sales funnel.

  • **Track total campaign spend**: Include AI voice bot platform fees, setup costs, phone numbers, and any additional software integrations. Don't forget staff time for initial configuration.

  • **Count qualified leads only**: Measure leads that meet your specific criteria (budget, timeline, decision-maker contact) rather than total conversations initiated.

  • **Factor in conversion rates**: AI voice bots typically achieve 15-25% higher lead qualification rates than traditional methods. [CallRail](https://www.callrail.com/blog/voice-ai-lead-generation) This improves your cost-per-qualified-lead significantly.

  • **Compare across channels**: Benchmark your AI voice bot cost-per-lead against email, PPC, and cold calling to identify your most efficient acquisition method.

  • **Monitor over time**: Lead costs typically decrease 30-40% after the first month as AI systems learn and optimize conversations.
