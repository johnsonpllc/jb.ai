This Week's Tip

Here's how to quickly calculate if an AI chatbot will pay for itself at your local service business.

  • **Track your missed calls**: Log how many calls go unanswered after 5 PM and on weekends for one week. Multiply by your average job value to see potential lost revenue.

  • **Calculate lead capture rate**: Assume your AI chatbot captures 30-40% of after-hours inquiries that would otherwise be lost. This is your baseline for new leads.

  • **Estimate conversion value**: If you close 20% of leads at $500 average job value, and capture 10 extra leads monthly, that's $1,000 in new revenue.

  • **Factor in cost savings**: AI handles initial screening 24/7, reducing time spent on qualifying calls during business hours.

  • **Compare to chatbot cost**: Most AI chatbots for local businesses cost $100-300/month. If you're generating $1,000+ in new revenue, your ROI is 300-900%.

  • **Add it up**: Revenue increase minus chatbot cost equals your monthly profit boost.
