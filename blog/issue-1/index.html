This Week's Tip

Here's your quick formula to calculate AI chatbot ROI and prove its value to your local service business.

  • **Track after-hours inquiries**: Monitor how many leads come in outside business hours (typically 60-70% of online inquiries happen after 5 PM) and multiply by your average customer value.

  • **Calculate response time savings**: Measure current lead response time vs. instant AI responses - every hour of delay reduces conversion chances by 10% [Harvard Business Review](https://hbr.org/2011/03/the-short-life-of-online-sales-leads).

  • **Count captured vs. lost leads**: Compare monthly leads before/after AI implementation - most businesses see 25-40% increase in qualified leads within 60 days.

  • **Factor in labor costs**: Calculate staff time saved on initial inquiries, appointment scheduling, and basic FAQs (typically 15-20 hours per week for local services).

  • **Simple ROI formula**: (Monthly revenue increase - monthly AI cost) ÷ monthly AI cost × 100 = your percentage ROI.
