This Week's Tip

Here's your quick formula to calculate chatbot ROI and prove its value to your local service business.

  • **Track after-hours inquiries**: Count missed calls and website visits outside business hours for one month—this is your baseline of lost opportunities.

  • **Estimate conversion rates**: Apply your typical lead-to-customer rate (usually 10-20% for local services) to those missed contacts to calculate potential lost revenue.

  • **Calculate monthly chatbot costs**: Add software subscription, setup fees, and maintenance time—typically $100-500/month for most local businesses.

  • **Project captured leads**: Chatbots typically capture 30-60% of after-hours inquiries that would otherwise be lost. [Drift](https://www.drift.com/blog/chatbot-stats/) reports 67% of consumers prefer chatbots for quick questions.

  • **Run the ROI math**: (Additional monthly revenue from captured leads - chatbot costs) ÷ chatbot costs × 100 = ROI percentage.

  • **Factor in 24/7 availability**: Unlike staff, chatbots work weekends, holidays, and late nights when competitors are offline.
