This Week's Tip

Here's your quick-and-dirty formula for calculating chatbot ROI that'll make your accountant smile.

  • **Start with your current after-hours missed calls** – Check your phone logs for calls between 6 PM-8 AM and weekends. Most service businesses miss 40-60% of these potential customers.

  • **Calculate average customer value** – Take last year's revenue divided by total customers. For most local services, this ranges from $300-2,000 per customer.

  • **Estimate chatbot capture rate** – AI chatbots typically convert 15-25% of after-hours visitors into qualified leads, compared to 0% when you're closed.

  • **Factor in your conversion rate** – If you normally close 30% of qualified leads, apply that same percentage to your new chatbot leads.

  • **Do the math** – (Monthly missed opportunities × chatbot capture rate × your close rate × average customer value) - chatbot cost = monthly ROI.

  • **Bonus points** – Track appointment bookings and FAQ deflection to boost those numbers even higher.
