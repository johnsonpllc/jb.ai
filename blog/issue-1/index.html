This Week's Tip

Here's how to quickly calculate whether an AI chatbot will pay for itself at your local service business.

  • **Track your missed calls**: Check your phone system for after-hours calls in the past 3 months, then multiply by your average conversion rate (typically 20-30% for service businesses)

  • **Calculate lost revenue**: Multiply those missed leads by your average job value—if you're missing 50 leads monthly at $300 average, that's $15,000 in potential revenue

  • **Factor in competitor capture**: [BirdEye found](https://birdeye.com/resources/reports/2023-consumer-expectations-report/) that 67% of customers will call a competitor if you don't respond within an hour

  • **Compare to chatbot costs**: Most AI chatbots run $200-800/month—if you're losing $10,000+ monthly to missed opportunities, the ROI is obvious

  • **Add voice capabilities**: AI voice bots can handle actual phone calls 24/7, capturing leads even when customers prefer calling over texting

If your calculation shows 3x+ return, implement immediately.
